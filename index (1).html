The Bronson Chicken Noodle

"The Crystal Clear Chicken Noodle"

Bronson's Chef Log: This is my ultimate soup. We are using Better Than Bouillon but keeping it 100% clear. No gross chunks, no ginger (yuck!), no zucchini, and absolutely no lemon peels! Just pure golden deliciousness.

Stuff You Need from the Garden & Pantry:

  • 🍗 2 Big Scoops: Better Than Bouillon Roasted Chicken Base
  • 💧 4 Cups: Purified water (keep it clean!)
  • 🌿 1 Sprig: Fresh green thyme or parsley from our amazing garden
  • 🥕 1 Whole: Organic carrot (Stamping these into star shapes is a pro move)
  • 🌱 1 Stalk: Crispy celery chopped into little half-moons
  • ⚫ 1 Pinch: Regular black pepper (No white pepper needed!)

How To Actually Make It Without Ruining It:

  1. Melt the Goop: Whisk your bouillon paste into the water until it completely disappears. Drop your garden herb sprig right into the liquid.
  2. The Whisper Simmer: Turn the stove to medium-low. Keep the bubbles tiny! If you let it go into a crazy rolling boil, the fats blast together and your broth gets permanently muddy. Toss in your pinch of black pepper.
  3. The Ultimate Trap: Pour the broth through a super fine strainer or a coffee filter into a clean pot. This traps all the tiny grit and herb sticks so it is perfectly translucent.
  4. The Separate Cook: Boil your star carrots and celery half-moons in a completely separate pot of water until soft, then drain them. Put them at the bottom of your bowl first. (Cook your noodles separately too!)
  5. Blast Off: Pour your crystal-clear golden broth over the veggies and noodles. Boom! Dinner is served.
